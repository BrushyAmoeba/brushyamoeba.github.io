About

What does Il Tegamino mean?

We get that question a lot, but we love sharing the definition.

Directly translated, Il Tegamino means "The Pan". To the Panzuto brothers, it means so much more. Il Tegamino is a tribute to their Mamma Rita. Rita collected tegaminos in her kitchen where Giuseppe and Salvator grew up in Napoli, Italy. She inspired their success into the culinary industry. Il tegamino is the long sought after result of the Panzuto brothers' American dream.

We are so excited about the IKl Tegamino project and can't wait to share what truly authentic Italian comfort food tastes like in the ambiance of the Panzuto family kitchen.

Stay tuned! Ciao! Grazie!

Location

The special history behind Il Tegamino's location

The history in Carmel-by-the-Sea is one of the reasons so many tourists love exploring the nooks, crannies, passages, and especially the courtyards of the quaint area.

Tucked behind the iconic Cottage of Sweets on Ocean Avenue, we think The Court of Golden Bough is one of the best hidden gems within Carmel-by-the-Sea.

History speaks for itself and we are thrilled to be part of the special courtyard's future.

Read about how the courtyard was one of the original hot spots of Carmel in this article by Edible Monterey Bay.
